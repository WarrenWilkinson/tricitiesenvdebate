About TriCities Environment Debate

TriCities Environment Debate is a debate between federal candidates from the electoral districts of Coquitlam-Port Coquitlam and Port Moody Coquitlam. The debate is being organized by a multi-partisan (non partisan?) committee. The debate will be about the environment and is affiliated with 100 debates.

The committee is open to participation by anyone from the community. Our goal is to create a debate that is fair to all parties and candidates so that different views on this important issue can be discussed. To join the committee or volunteer to help us, please contact us.
